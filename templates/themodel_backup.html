Labeling Song Lyrics

I compiled a list of artists from Billboard year-end top artist lists. I used Spotify's API to get genres for each artist on that list. I built and analyzed a graph that shows the genres that are shared for any given artist. I found clusters of genres to get more general classifications: Rock, Hip-Hop, and Country.

I used the artist genre labels and these clusters to label the genre of song lyrics I scraped from the web.

Building a Predictive Model

The genre of a song is a good approximation of its feel.

I trained a multinomial Naive Bayes classifier to predict genres from song lyrics. For The Anything Mixtape this is extended to predict the genre for any piece of text.

Picking Playlist Songs

Once a description is classified to a genre songs are selected. I use K-means clustering to find songs that cover similar topics as the input text.

If you give me your Spotify username, I can automatically create a playlist for you within your profile.
